Frequently Asked Questions

Here you will find answers to common questions.

The Result Management System is designed to provide students, staff, and administrators with an efficient way to manage and access academic results.

Students can log in using their credentials to view and download their results from the Student Dashboard.

Staff can upload student marks either by entering them individually or by uploading a file from the Staff Dashboard.

Administrators have the ability to manage credentials for students and staff, ensuring secure access to the system.
